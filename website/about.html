Our Story

ExperienceLink was founded with a simple idea: every student deserves the opportunity to gain real-world experience before graduation.

We noticed that many talented students struggle to land their first internship because most positions require prior experience. At the same time, companies have countless small projects that could benefit from fresh perspectives and eager talent.
